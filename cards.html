Cards

How they work

You can swipe left or right to progress forward into the story

If a back button is implemented

You can press it to move backwards

You can also press that little arrow in the bottom left at any point

but both buttons are optional, you can also swipe all the way through

and back to the beginning!

I recommend spacing your cards into 3 sections

Each of these sections should fully contain their content

This is achieved with display:flex and overlap:hidden

Smaller screens will run out of space in overly wordy situations

Especially in trying to manage between portrait and landscape views
